This guide is for team members whose admin has invited them. You'll do this
once; afterwards you just use Claude as usual.

## Add the connector to Claude

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Claude's connector settings">
    In Claude, open **Settings → Connectors** and choose
    **Add custom connector**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste your company's connector address">
    Your admin shared a link that looks like
    `https://…/mcp`. Paste it in and click **Add**.

    *[screenshot: Claude add-connector dialog]*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect and sign in">
    Click **Connect**. A sign-in page opens — choose **Sign in with SSO**
    and use your normal work account (the same one you use for email).

    *[screenshot: gateway sign-in page]*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link your own account (first time only)">
    If your company connected a system like HubSpot, you may be asked to
    approve access to **your** account there once. This is how your actions
    stay yours — the AI acts as you, never as someone else.
  </Step>
</Steps>

That's it. Ask Claude to look up a customer, update a deal, draft an email —
whatever your role allows.

## When something is "Denied by policy"

Sometimes Claude will tell you an action was denied, or simply won't have
the ability you asked for. **That's the system working**, not breaking:
your admin decided that action isn't part of your role.

If you believe you need it, ask your admin — granting it takes them under a
minute, and the change is recorded.

## Good to know

- **Your login is your identity.** Everything the AI does on your behalf is
  logged under your name — same as if you'd done it by hand.
- **You never handle passwords or keys.** Signing in with your work account
  is all there is.
- **If your role changes**, your abilities update immediately — no
  reconnecting needed.
